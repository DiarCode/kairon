1. **Live order flow works.** Kairon successfully connected to Bybit testnet, fetched 1m bars via REST polling, ran the ComprehensiveStrategy, submitted real market orders, and recorded orders/decisions/heartbeats to per-symbol SQLite stores.

## Notes / Next Steps

- Fix the orchestrator's Unicode stdout handling so future multi-symbol runs stream cleanly (set `PYTHONIOENCODING=utf-8` and/or strip pybit arrow characters before printing).
- Consider lowering the cooldown threshold or widening signal thresholds for short test sessions so more symbols actually trade.
- Investigate the Bybit testnet `30208` price-limit rejection on ETH — this does not occur on mainnet and is likely a testnet liquidity/protection artifact.
