## Methodology notes

- **Stop-level exit approximation:** when the exchange's attached SL/TP closes a position but the WS close fill does not drain (a testnet race), the orchestrator reconciles the local mirror to flat at the *stop level* (the price where the attached SL/TP sat), not the crossing price. This matches the exchange fill and avoids overstating the realized loss. Tagged `software_stop_reconcile` in `live_events`.
- **Orphan stop cleanup:** every close path (software stop, reconcile, TP/SL fill, manual flatten) calls `cancel_all(symbol)` in attach_stops mode so a stale attached TP/SL conditional cannot fire on a future move with no position to close.
- **Risk-cap guard:** after any quantity rounding, the sizer recomputes the implied risk and skips a trade whose implied risk would exceed `risk_per_trade * (1 + risk_cap_tol)`; sub-min-lot signals skip unless `--allow-min-lot-overshoot` is set and the overshoot stays within tol.
